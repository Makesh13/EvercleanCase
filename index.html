EVERCLEAN

EverClean is a unique app, which helps to find and remove photo and video duplicates. It will definitely save a huge amount or nerves and hours, as you don’t have to perform the cleaning manually.

You need to make only one scan to find similar files and delete them right away. In case you take dozens of identical photos to choose the best one afterwards, then this app is just for you!

Main features of our app:

  • The program can detect and delete all the duplicate photos and screenshots automatically. The same goes for blurred images, which have imperfections.

  • By using EverClean, you can restore a lot of space on the device. As the result, you won’t get those annoying notifications, that the storage is full.

  • It’s incredibly easy to delete screenshots! It doesn’t matter why did you make them. We can help you to remove everything - from memes to book pages. Love taking many screenshots? Not a problem.

Why is it so simple and comfortable?

In case you don’t want to waste extra time on previewing and checking, then you can store all the important files to a special folder. EverClean won’t have access to it.

Nothing will be deleted without letting you know. It’s also possible to preview photos and videos before confirming the removal.

Due to the smart scanning algorithm, duplicate photos and screenshots can be found in an instant.

EverClean lets you remove each file taken in a certain place. By running it, you can quickly delete all the Instagram photos.
